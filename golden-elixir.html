Golden Elixir

"Warm golden sweetness blended with deep sensual luxury."

A warm, luxurious fragrance that feels smooth, sensual, and addictive. Golden Elixir opens with a radiant burst of saffron and spiced citrus, lifted by the brightness of bergamot. The heart blossoms into a rich, velvety accord of amber, rose, and tonka bean — deeply intimate and utterly captivating. The base lingers endlessly with vanilla, a light oud accord, musk, and patchouli, leaving a trail of warm golden sweetness that clings to skin like a memory.

Golden Elixir was born from a desire to bottle warmth itself — the golden hour of a Middle Eastern evening, the sweetness of spice markets, the soft glow of amber candlelight. Our perfumer wove together precious saffron from the East, delicate rose, and a rare light oud accord to create a fragrance that is at once immediately wearable and deeply luxurious. Finished with vanilla, musk, and patchouli, it is addictive from the very first breath. Golden Elixir is warmth you wear.
Golden Elixir shines brightest when worn close to the skin. Apply to wrists, the inside of elbows, and the base of the throat for a warm, enveloping sillage. Perfect for autumn and winter months, or any evening when you want to feel effortlessly luxurious. The amber and vanilla base make it an exceptional choice for date nights, celebrations, and intimate gatherings.
