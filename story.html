The House of Amora Noir

Our Story

Chapter I

The Heritage of Arabian Perfumery

Amora Noir was born in the heart of the dunes, inspired by the ancient spice trails, the sweet burning incense of local majlises, and the profound depth of natural Oud. We sought to capture this timeless olfactive heritage and refine it with the sophistication of modern French perfumery.

Every raw ingredient tells a story. Our saffron is sourced from the high plains where the soil is rich, our roses are harvested at dawn when their petals carry the highest concentration of fragrance oil, and our Cambodian Oud is aged to achieve a majestic, smoky warmth that sits at the center of our creation.

Chapter II

The Craft of Hand-Blending

We believe that true luxury cannot be mass-produced. At our atelier, each batch of Amora Noir perfume is individually blended and left to mature in controlled darkness. This slow maceration allows the natural oils, resins, and absolutes to fuse together, developing a complex depth that synthetic perfumery can never replicate.

Our perfumers spend months, sometimes years, perfecting a single formula. It is a meticulous balance of volatile top notes that capture your attention, warm heart notes that set the mood, and heavy base notes that anchor the perfume, ensuring an extraordinary sillage that lasts all day and into the night.
