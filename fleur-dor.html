Fleur d'Or

"A graceful floral scent that feels romantic and timeless."

Soft, feminine, and irresistibly elegant. Fleur d'Or opens with a bright, juicy accord of pear and mandarin, lifted by a delicate touch of bergamot — fresh as a garden at first light. The heart unfolds into a lush bouquet of rose, jasmine, and peony, romantic and full in bloom. The fragrance settles gracefully onto a base of white musk, vanilla, and soft amber, leaving a tender, lingering trail that feels like a whisper of silk against skin. Timeless femininity, captured in a bottle.

Fleur d'Or was born from the romance of a blooming garden — the soft morning light filtering through petals, the sweetness of ripe fruit on the breeze, and the gentle warmth of a love that never fades. Our perfumer wove together the freshness of pear and mandarin with an opulent heart of rose, jasmine, and peony, each chosen for their natural radiance and depth. Anchored in white musk, vanilla, and soft amber, Fleur d'Or is a fragrance of grace — romantic, timeless, and effortlessly beautiful.
Apply to pulse points — wrists, neck, and behind the ears — for optimal diffusion. For longevity, layer with an unscented moisturiser before application. Fleur d'Or is a day-to-evening fragrance, ideal for occasions where elegance is the dress code. Store upright, away from direct sunlight and extreme heat, to preserve the integrity of the scent.
