Royal Oud Noir

"A rich, royal fragrance for a strong and confident presence."

Deep, bold, and unmistakably Arabian. Royal Oud Noir opens with a commanding burst of bergamot, precious saffron, and black pepper — an opening that announces itself with authority. The heart unfolds into an intoxicating accord of rose, jasmine, and incense, steeped in the tradition of the finest oriental perfumery. At its core, pure agarwood oud reigns supreme, softened by amber, musk, and sandalwood — a dry-down of extraordinary depth and presence. For those who wear power with grace.

Royal Oud Noir is a tribute to the ancient perfumery traditions of Arabia, where oud — the wood of the gods — was revered above all other ingredients. Our master perfumer sought to capture that timeless power: the sacred smoke of incense drifting through a royal majlis, the intoxicating bloom of rose and jasmine at dusk, and the deep, resinous warmth of pure agarwood at its heart. Grounded in amber, musk, and sandalwood, this is a fragrance of presence, heritage, and quiet dominance. A crown, bottled.
Royal Oud Noir is a statement fragrance — powerful, long-lasting, and deeply sillage-rich. Apply sparingly to warm pulse points: wrists, neck, and chest. A little goes a long way. Ideal for evening occasions, formal gatherings, and moments that demand presence. The fragrance evolves beautifully over 8–12 hours, deepening as it warms to your skin.
